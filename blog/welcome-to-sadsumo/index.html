Welcome to SadSumo Consulting - Here's What We're Building

January 15, 2025 · 4 min read

Hey there! Welcome to the SadSumo Consulting blog. I'm really excited to share what we're working on and where we're headed.

So here's the thing—I've been thinking a lot about how AI and modern technology are changing everything, but there's this massive gap between what's possible and what most small and medium-sized businesses can actually access. You've got these incredible tools out there, but if you're running a logistics company in Savannah or a healthcare practice in the Lowcountry, you probably don't have the time (or frankly, the patience) to figure out how to make AI work for your business.

That's exactly what we're setting out to solve with SadSumo Consulting.

What We're All About#

Our mission is pretty straightforward: making artificial intelligence and advanced automation accessible to businesses who don't have the time or technical expertise to navigate AI's complexity. We want to turn cutting-edge technology into straightforward business solutions that actually work for you.

We're focusing on three main areas:

Mobile AI and Web Applications - Custom solutions built specifically for your business needs. Not cookie-cutter templates, but actual applications that solve your unique challenges.

Power Platform Training and Implementation - Microsoft's Power Platform is incredibly powerful, but it can be overwhelming. We help you unlock its potential through hands-on training and implementation support.

Digital Transformation Strategy - This is the big picture stuff. How do you evolve your business processes to take advantage of what technology offers today? We help you figure that out and make it happen.

Who We're Here to Serve#

We're laser-focused on small- and medium-sized companies right here in Southern Georgia and the Lowcountry—places like Savannah, Pooler, Rincon, Bluffton, and Hilton Head. Whether you're in logistics, manufacturing, healthcare, professional services, or retail, we're here to help.

The businesses we work with are doing incredible things, but they often get stuck when it comes to technology. Either the solutions are too basic (just a simple website when you need something more), or they're quoted enterprise prices that don't make sense for a mid-sized operation. We're filling that gap.

Why Now? Why Here?#

Here's something exciting—the Southern Georgia tech sector has been growing like crazy. We're talking over 20% annual growth, contributing $1.2 billion to the Savannah economy alone. There are projections for 3,000+ new tech jobs coming, and since 2018, we've seen 50+ startups pop up in the area.

This isn't just about growth for growth's sake. It means there's genuine demand for what we do, and more importantly, it means the businesses around us are ready to innovate. They're looking for partners who can help them navigate this new landscape.

What Makes Us Different#

One thing I'm particularly proud of: our team includes consultants with U.S. Army cybersecurity experience. When we build solutions—especially for healthcare, finance, or any industry where security is critical—we're bringing enterprise-grade security practices to the table. That's not something you typically get from your average web development shop, and it's absolutely essential in today's environment.

What's Next#

This blog is going to be our space to share what we're learning, showcase the cool projects we're working on, and hopefully provide some value to the business community here in Southern Georgia and beyond. We'll talk about AI implementation, share tips on digital transformation, dive into case studies, and keep you updated on what's happening in the local tech scene.

I'm genuinely excited about what we're building here. If you're a business owner who's been thinking "there's got to be a better way to do this" when it comes to your technology and processes, let's talk. We're here to help make that happen.

Thanks for reading, and welcome to the SadSumo journey!

Interested in learning more about how we can help your business? Contact us or visit our web page.
